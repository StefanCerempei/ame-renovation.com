AME Rénovation is a professional renovation company that has specialized in bespoke interior transformations since 2015.
Based in France and operating across regions including the Côte d'Azur, Provence, and Paris, the company provides a comprehensive range of services such as demolition, painting and finishing, electrical work (NF C15-100 certified), plumbing, and flooring.

The website features an interactive budget simulator for instant project estimates and emphasizes a "turnkey" approach, managing projects from initial study to final delivery. With a multilingual team and a 10-year warranty (assurance décennale) on their work, they focus on high-end finishes and transparent project management for residential and commercial renovations.
